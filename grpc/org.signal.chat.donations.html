rpc RedeemReceipt
Request: org.signal.chat.donations.RedeemReceiptRequest
Response: org.signal.chat.donations.RedeemReceiptResponse

Redeem a receipt acquired from Subscriptions.CreateSubscriptionReceiptCredentials to add a badge to the account. After successful redemption, profile responses will include the corresponding badge (if configured as visible) until the expiration time on the receipt.
